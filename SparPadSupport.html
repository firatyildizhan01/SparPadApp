About SparPad

SparPad is a professional boxing training simulator that brings professional training to your fingertips. With interactive punching combinations, progressive difficulty levels, and real-time performance tracking, SparPad helps you improve your boxing skills anytime, anywhere.

Key Features:

  • Real-time training experience with visual feedback
  • Interactive punching combinations
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Performance tracking and analytics
  • Motion sensor integration for precise movement detection

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply download SparPad from the App Store, grant the necessary permissions (camera and motion sensors), and follow the onboarding process. You'll be ready to start training in minutes!

What permissions does SparPad need?

SparPad requires the following permissions to provide the best training experience:

  • Camera: For real-time training experience with visual feedback
  • Photo Library: To save training session screenshots and progress photos
  • Motion Sensors: For precise movement detection during training

Is SparPad suitable for beginners?

Yes! SparPad is designed for all skill levels. Our progressive difficulty system adapts to your experience level, making it perfect for both beginners and advanced practitioners.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:

  • Open Settings on your device
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select SparPad
  • Tap Cancel Subscription

Privacy & Terms
