SparPad - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: SparPad is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Training preferences, fitness goals, skill level
  • Payment Information: Processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store (we do not store payment card details)

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

Data Type Purpose Storage
Training Session Data Track your performance, punches, combinations, timing Stored locally on your device
Device Information Device model, OS version, app version Used for analytics and debugging
Motion Sensor Data Detect and track punch movements during training Processed in real-time, not stored
Camera Data Provide real-time visual feedback during training Processed in real-time, not stored unless you save photos
Usage Analytics Improve app performance and user experience Anonymized and aggregated
Error Logs (Sentry) Identify and fix bugs and crashes Stored on secure servers

1.3 Camera and Motion Sensors

Camera Access: SparPad uses your device camera to provide real-time training feedback and visual guidance. Camera feed is:

  • Processed locally on your device in real-time
  • NOT recorded or transmitted to our servers
  • NOT stored unless you explicitly save a screenshot to your photo library
  • Only active when you are using training features

Motion Sensors: We access your device's accelerometer and gyroscope to:

  • Detect punch movements and measure intensity
  • Provide accurate training feedback
  • Enhance your training experience

Motion data is processed in real-time and is not stored or transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Maintain the App: Enable core training features and track your progress
  • Personalize Your Experience: Customize training programs based on your skill level
  • Process Subscriptions: Manage your premium membership and billing
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Send Notifications: Training reminders, progress updates (only if you opt-in)
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Respond to legal requests and prevent illegal activity

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: Training history, saved combinations, preferences stored on your device
  • Cloud Storage (Optional): If you enable cloud sync, data is encrypted and stored on secure servers
  • Analytics Servers: Anonymized usage data via Expo and Sentry

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

SparPad does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the app:

Service Provider Purpose Data Shared
Apple App Store / Google Play Payment processing and subscription management Purchase information, subscription status
Sentry Error tracking and crash reporting Device info, app version, error logs
Expo App updates and analytics Device type, OS version, anonymous usage data

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoena, court order)
  • Requests from government authorities
  • Protection of our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Investigation of potential fraud or security issues

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correct Your Data: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export Your Data: Download your training history and progress
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics, notifications, or camera/sensor access

5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

In-App Settings: Manage privacy preferences in Settings → Privacy

Contact Us: Email akilab.apps@gmail.com for data requests

Delete Account: Settings → Account → Delete Account (this action is permanent)

5.3 Device Permissions

You can manage app permissions in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → SparPad → Permissions
  • Android: Settings → Apps → SparPad → Permissions

Permissions we request:

  • Camera: For real-time training feedback (optional)
  • Motion & Fitness: For punch detection (optional)
  • Photo Library: To save training screenshots (optional)
  • Notifications: For training reminders (optional)

6. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: SparPad is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using SparPad, you consent to such transfers.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide app services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Account Deletion: When you delete your account, your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Backup copies are deleted within 90 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SparPad uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Local Storage: To save your preferences and training data
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage tracking to improve the app
  • Session Management: To keep you logged in

We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies or trackers.

10. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying an in-app notice

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Note: SparPad does NOT sell personal information.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

Legal Basis: We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (e.g., camera access, notifications)
  • Contract performance (e.g., providing app services)
  • Legal obligations (e.g., financial records)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving the app, fraud prevention)

14. Do Not Track Signals

SparPad respects Do Not Track (DNT) signals. We do not track users across third-party websites or apps.

16. Supervisory Authority

If you are in the EEA and believe we have not addressed your privacy concerns adequately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Summary: SparPad respects your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide training services, do not sell your information, and give you full control over your data. Camera and sensor data are processed locally in real-time and not stored. You can delete your account and data at any time.
